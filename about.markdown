I am a **Physical AI Engineer** with 3 years of post-MSc professional experience and a 14-year technical trajectory in software engineering, specializing in the development of autonomous systems that perceive, reason, and act in complex, real-world environments. My work lives at the intersection of **Machine Perception**, **Distributed Systems**, and **Embedded AI**. As a polyglot engineer, I operate across the full robotics stack with strong experience in designing robust model lifecycles for latency-critical applications.

**Core Impact Areas:**
- **Scale & Optimization:** Architected scalable end-to-end systems for edge AI, achieving a 50× reduction in inference latency and accelerating data engineering workflows by 100× using Ray.
- **Innovative Research:** Developed novel synthetic training data generation approaches and human-attention-inspired perception pipelines for autonomous multi-agent systems.
- **Technical Leadership:** Led cross-functional efforts to transition experimental AI prototypes into robust, production-ready solutions at Siemens while mentoring junior engineers in best practices.
- **Competitive Excellence:** Recognized as a top-tier competitor in autonomous driving, securing 1st and 3rd place finishes in international F1Tenth competitions at IROS and ICRA.
